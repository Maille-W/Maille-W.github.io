"Basic Fruit" refers to fruit that is commonly eaten, seen in grocery stores, or perched in your parent's fruit bowl atop their counter.

Examples of this fruit would include:

As pictured:

  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • Apples
  • Strawberries

  • Grapes
  • Pears
  • Cherries
  • Lemons
  • Peaches
  • Pineapples
