"Eccentric Fruit" refers to fruits that are unique, not typically part of our region, and arent normally found in grocery stores.

Examples of this fruit would include:

As pictured:

  • Mangosteen
  • Loquat
  • Durian
  • Ackee

More fruits that fit into this category:

  • Persimmon
  • Guava
  • Lychee
  • Passionfruit
  • Pitaya
  • Starfruit
  • Jackfruit
