Berries are differentiated from other fruits by their botanical classification, originating from a single ovary of a single flower, typically containing multiple seeds within their flesh, and characterized by a soft outer skin and high levels of antioxidants and vitamins.

Examples of this fruit would include:

As pictured:

  • Blackberries
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Cranberries

More fruits that fit into this category:

  • Huckle berry
  • Elder berry
  • Lingon berry
  • Acai berry
  • Salmon berry
